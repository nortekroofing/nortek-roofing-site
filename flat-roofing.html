Flat Roofing Systems

We provide complete 2-ply SBS flat roofing assemblies for commercial, industrial, and residential applications. Our systems are designed for durability, watertight protection, and long-term performance.

  • Two-layer torch-applied SBS membrane for redundancy and watertight security
  • Compatible with tapered insulation systems for positive drainage
  • Full slope packages and polyiso insulation options available
  • Straight-to-deck application available with flame-free systems
  • Reinforced base sheet with protective cap sheet finish
