Commercial Roofing Systems

We focus exclusively on proven, long-service commercial and industrial roofing assemblies. Our installations are carried out with precision and in strict compliance with manufacturer specifications and industry standards.

2-Ply SBS Torch-Applied Membrane

A durable, flexible system designed to handle the demands of large-scale flat and low-slope projects.

  • Two-layer torch-applied SBS membrane for redundancy and watertight security
  • Heat-welded seams with controlled application to ensure full adhesion
  • Compatible with tapered insulation systems for positive drainage
  • Reinforced base sheet with protective cap sheet finish
  • Typical service life: 25–30 years with proper maintenance

True Standing Seam Metal (24ga)

A premium roofing system fabricated and installed for longevity, strength, and clean architectural lines.

  • 24-gauge steel panels, roll-formed on site for continuous length runs
  • Mechanically seamed for superior watertight performance without exposed fasteners
  • Custom-fabricated flashings produced in-house for precise detailing
  • Thermal expansion accommodated through floating clip systems
  • Typical service life: 40–50 years with minimal maintenance
